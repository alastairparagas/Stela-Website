What Is Stela?

A vision for the future

Stela started with the duo of High School students - Alastair Paragas and Anil Jason. Together, they teamed up to grow a team of talented developers and designers bent on shaping products and services that seek to give people back control of technology.

Web, Mobile, and Desktop App Development

Stela prides itself in creating high quality apps for both the web, mobile (Android and iOS), and desktop (Windows, Macintosh, Linux) platforms. We also can design websites customized to your needs, whether it be for an individual flair or business pursuit.

Dedication for perfection

All our products and services undergo continous and unparalleled scrutiny. We utilize the full scope of the web, from virtual meetings and assignment boards to our very own in-house software (and artificial intelligence to boot) to get the job done.

Post-Script: Experience

Stela (formerly Forfront Technology) was founded back on September 2010, during a conversation between founders Alastair Paragas and Anil Jason during an Engineering class. Ever since then, the two have been inseparable, staying late night coding applications, My Right To Play being the first of them. Ever since then, they grew to join and compete at technological competitions. Coming to their adult years, the growing team has set its sights to creating a mature and driven company.

Our Work Process

Stela is driven to create beautiful products and pursue perfect service. We utilize a creative but order and structured work process to ensure the quality and timeliness of our tasks.

1

Communication

We establish virtual group meetings through Google Hangouts to communicate about key problems and ideas at hand. Meetings are held bi-monthly or on-demand.

2

Kanban

We use pen/paper to draw up our ideas. Utilizing the Kanban system of organization, we design steps of a solution and assign them to the appropriate members.

3

Git, Bower, Gulp, Repeat

Utilizing the full scope of tools available in the web, from Git revision management system, to automating tools like Gulp, we keep the project/task clean and organized.

4

Maintenance

With the power of Social Media such as Youtube, Facebook, and Instagram, we disseminate conversation from the community to get tips on how to improve our product/service.
